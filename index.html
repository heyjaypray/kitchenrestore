Our Process

Step 1

Choose your door style. We have a wide selection of door styles that can be mixed and matched to enhance your home's design and decor.

Step 2

Choose your color. We are known for our unique finishing so be sure to think "outside the box" here and make your cabinet color be the focal point of your kitchen.

Step 3

Choose the accessories you want to simplify your life and help to keep you orgainized. Don't forget the special finishing touches to make your refaced cabinets look custom made, such as mouldings and toekicks.

Step 4

Choose your new countertop material. We do it all from laminates in all colors, to solid surface materials, to granite. We install it and get you hooked up with plumbing and electrical. The only thing left for you to do is find a vase of flowers to decorate your new refaced kitchen

Our services

Free Consultation

Our first meeting we will get to know one another and what your needs are. An inspection of the existing cabinet boxes will be done, and suggestions made as to their appropriateness for refacing. We will discuss any other design needs that you may have such as, would you like an island installed? Do you have enough storage? How about a pantry? Do you need a matching hutch in the dining room? How about an entertainment center? From there we will contact you with a price for your reFaced kitchen.

Samples

We will bring an arrangement of cabinet samples for you to choose from, including some with our custom finishes to give you an idea of how they fit with your decor. We also have many photos of past customers that you may like to look though to get an idea of what we can do for you. While we are at it, take a look at our countertop samples too, to get a feel for what your new kitchen could possibly look like once it is all manufactured and installed.

Manufacturing

Unlike most refacing companies who order stock cabinet doors and drawers already pre-finished, you will be getting a finishing service for the same price, because we do it ourselves. Did you just want to match an existing door color and style? We can probably do that. Our box fronts are also finished at the same time as the doors so that you can get a beautiful refacing job that looks as if the cabihets were custom made and designed for your kitchen alone.

Installation

Our installers have years of experience upgrading and installing kitchens. They've seen it all, which is a benefit to you because when any unforseen problems arise, they are able to think quickly on their feet and fix it with little or no down time. Other refacers have to wait for their manufacturers to send replacement hardware/materials, but we are the manufacturer so we can fix surprises as they come up.
